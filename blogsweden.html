The Castle in Kalmar

The castle in the Swedish city of Kalmar is one of the best preserved Renaissance castles in Northern Europe.

The thick walls and massive towers remind you that Kalmar Castle was above all a fortress. Here at Kalmarsund, Sweden's border was defended for centuries.

Stockholms Underground

The subway in Stockholm, the "longest gallery in the world"

You can find sculptures, mosaics, paintings, installations, inscriptions and reliefs from the 1950s to the 2000s in most Stockholm subway stations. This gigantic art gallery is a well-known landmark of the Swedish capital.
