The Chapel bridge

The Chapel Bridge is the oldest covered wooden bridge in Europe.

Unfortunately, the bridge fell victim to a conflagration in August 1993 and destroyed a big part of the bridge, including 78 of the 111 famous images and a lot of spiders.

Delicious Vermicelles

Vermicelles is a Swiss dessert made from chestnut puree.

It's one of my favorite desserts. The chestnut puree is served with meringue and whipped cream. This dessert is also available as a sundae with vanilla ice cream. You can also use it to make delicious cakes, pies and other dessert variations yourself.
