Fire & Ice

Iceland, the island of fire and ice.
It is still very wilde. Many of the roads are still gravel roads, specially around the Westfjörds.
You need also during the summer time a good warm jacket and don't beliefe any Travle Guides that a umbrella is a good idea in Iceland. Bring a good rain jacket with you.

A bath in a hot stream

One of my best experiences was swimming in the hot river in Reykjadaldur.

However, the river cannot be reached by car. You have to plan a little hike that gives this trip that extra something.
Fortunately the hiking trail is one of the few that is well developed and maintained.

The Viking Festival

In Hafnarfjörður is once a year a Viking Festival in the very little Viking Village.

The small Viking village consists of the famous Viking Hotel, the Viking Restaurant and a few very very small houses built in the Viking style. So don't expect to much.
